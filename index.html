ABOUT COMPANY

SV Agri strives to create value across the food supply chain and food processing sector by blending knowledge of agriculture, food processing and technology. We adopt a ‘solutions’ approach to design innovations and catalyze change.

With the intention of bridging gap between potato value chain and snacks processing industry, Siddhivinayak Agri Processing Pvt. Ltd. was set up in the year 2008. Since then, we have come a long way by creating better livelihood for farmers, providing reliable supply base to food manufacturers and improving efficiency and enhancing productivity of production lines.
